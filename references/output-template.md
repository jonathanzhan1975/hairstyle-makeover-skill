# Output Template

Use this structure for user-facing results.

## Short recommendation format

```md
## Photo usability
- usable / usable with caution / not reliable
- one-sentence reason

## What stands out
- face and hair summary
- current hairstyle summary

## Recommendation
### Perfect
- style name
- why it works

### Good
- style name
- why it works

### Less ideal
- style name
- why it is weaker
```

## Full recommendation format

```md
## Photo usability
## Head, face, and hair read
## Hairstyle strategy
## Ranked recommendations
## What to avoid
## Barber wording
## Edit prompts
## Try-on image plan or output
```

## Tone rules

- Write for ordinary users, not stylists.
- Keep the language calm and confident.
- Explain tradeoffs without sounding harsh.
- Prefer "this is a stronger fit" over "this is wrong".
- Keep recommendation labels visually clear.

## Small-card wording

Useful short lines:
- `最稳`
- `更显精神`
- `更有型`
- `更保守`
- `更适合日常`
- `拍照更出片`

Use these sparingly and only when the user wants a social-card feel.
